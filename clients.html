CASE STUDY

From Regional Leader to Global Institution

The Challenge

  • Governance structure limiting growth beyond family management
  • Cultural dilution fears preventing strategic acquisitions
  • Next-generation leaders unsure of their roles

Our Approach

We designed a governance framework that balanced family control with professional management, created cultural integration protocols for acquisitions, and developed a 10-year leadership development program for next-generation family members.
